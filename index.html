About

The idea behind WeatherNow was sparked by a need for a simplified, user-friendly weather app.

Inspired by the idea of helping travelers worldwide not only know the weather of their destination but also compare it with the weather in their current location, making it easier for them to plan accordingly.

Timeline

  • Idea Formation - 1 week

    Conceptualized a weather app focused on real-time updates and comparison tools.

  • Development & API Integration - 3 weeks
